Features

Everything your browser
bookmarks wish they could do

Simple, fast, and thoughtfully designed to keep your saved links actually useful.

⚡
Save in seconds
Paste a URL and it's saved. No friction, no complicated forms — just the link and an optional note to remind you why you saved it.
🔍
Find anything instantly
Search by name across all your saved links. No more scrolling through hundreds of bookmarks trying to remember where something was.
📋
One-tap copy
Hit copy on any saved link to instantly get the URL on your clipboard — ready to share, open, or paste wherever you need it.
✏️
Edit anytime
Update the name, URL, or note on any saved link at any time. Your link library grows smarter as you add context over time.
🔒
Yours alone
Every link is private to your account. Nobody else can see, access, or modify what you've saved. Your vault, your rules.
🌐
Access anywhere
Your links live in the cloud, not on one device. Log in from your phone, laptop, or tablet and your entire collection is right there.
